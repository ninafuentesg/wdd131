About Peru

Peru is located on the west coast of South America, known for its ancient history, vibrant culture, and breathtaking natural landscapes.

From the Amazon rainforest to the Andes, and from the Pacific coast to the valleys, Peru has wonders for every traveler.

Peruvian Culture

Peru has a rich cultural heritage with traditions dating back thousands of years, including festivals, music, and crafts.
